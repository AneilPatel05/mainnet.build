Welcome to Swap.Online — free decentralized hot cross-chain wallet based on Atomic Swaps technology (peer-to-peer exchange between two blockchains with no third parties).

Here you can store, send, and exchange BTC, ETH, EOS, USDT, and ERC-20 tokens in a safe and decentralized manner.

Swap.Online have the ultimate level of decentralization because we don't store users’ crypto or private keys.

You don't need an additional plug-in installation or application download. Your wallets are created automatically when you open https://swap.online website. What you need to get started

You can import keys from your existing crypto wallets, or buy some crypto and send it to your Swap.Online wallets from popular exchanges.

By the way, if an exchange does not provide you with the private keys from your wallet that means the exchange is centralized.

Swap.Online creates your private keys and never holds them anywhere except inside the browser on your device. You can trust swap.online code as it is served via HTTPS using Cloudflare provider which checks code integrity.

However, with big power comes big responsibility: as we don't store your keys, we won't be able to restore your wallet in case you clear your browser's storage, change the device or lose your wallet.

Please, please, please, download your keys and store them somewhere safe!
